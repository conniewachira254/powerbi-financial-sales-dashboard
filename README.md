# 📊 Financial & Sales Analytics Dashboard (P&L Focused) – Power BI

---

## 🧭 Project Overview
This Power BI project delivers a comprehensive **Financial and Sales Performance Dashboard** built on a Profit & Loss (P&L) structure. It transforms raw sales data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making across revenue, cost, and profitability dimensions.

The dashboard is designed for business stakeholders, finance teams, and operations managers to monitor performance, identify trends, and optimize profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track total revenue performance over time  
- Analyze Cost of Sales (COS) and gross profitability  
- Evaluate Gross Margin (GM) and GM%  
- Identify top-performing and underperforming stores/products  
- Enable interactive exploration of business performance  

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Power Query (ETL)  
- Data Modeling (Star Schema approach)  
- Excel / CSV as data source  

---

## 📊 Key Features & Insights

### 📈 Revenue Performance
- Monthly and quarterly revenue trends  
- Seasonal pattern detection  

### 💰 Profitability Analysis
- Gross Margin (GM) and Gross Margin %  
- Cost of Sales impact on profitability  

### 🏬 Store Performance
- Store-level revenue comparison  
- Identification of high and low-performing stores  

### 🛍️ Product Performance
- Top-selling products analysis  
- Product contribution to overall revenue  

### ⚠️ Business Insights
- Identification of revenue drivers  
- Detection of cost inefficiencies  
- Margin leakage analysis  

---

## 🧮 Key DAX Measures
Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Revenue])

Cost of Sales = SUM(Sales[Cost])

Gross Margin = [Total Sales] - [Cost of Sales]

Gross Margin % = DIVIDE([Gross Margin], [Total Sales])

This dashboard enables:
